METHODOLOGY & NOTES
Rules applied to build the season data
Source: 24 original Charter Oak CC tee sheet PDFs (May 2 – July 19, 2026), parsed directly from each PDF's text layer using coordinate-based column detection, rebuilt July 21, 2026.
RuleApplication
Time WindowOnly tee times at 10:50 AM or earlier are included as "regular play," applied uniformly to every date including tournament days.
Tournament DatesJune 27 (Invitational Tournament, course closed all day) and July 11 (Club Championship Weekend) are full-day club tournaments, but their pre-10:50 AM tee times are still counted under the uniform time-window rule above rather than excluded outright.
Checksum ValidationEvery PDF's extracted player count was verified against that PDF's own stated "(N Players)" header count. All 24 dates matched exactly (100%).
Name ReconstructionPDF text extraction sometimes glues two adjacent players' names together or splits a name fragment across a column boundary; both patterns are detected and repaired automatically before counting.
Guest CategoriesGuest, Family Guest, and Comp are grouped as Guest for category/member classification purposes.
Guest Fee Definition"Guest Fees" counts only fee-paying categories (Guest + Family Guest = 111). Comp (complimentary, 127 rounds) is tracked separately since no fee is charged.
Round DefinitionEach player appearance in a qualifying regular-play tee time counts as one round.
Both Days, Same WeekendEach of the 12 Saturday/Sunday pairs (May 2–3 through July 18–19) is checked individually per member; the Member Round Totals column counts how many of those 12 weekends a member played both days.
